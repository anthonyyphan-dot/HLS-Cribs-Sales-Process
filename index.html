Keep them engaged

Prompt

While walking through each lender, keep checking in so the customer is following along, not just listening:

  • "What rate can you see?"
  • "Have you banked with XYZ before?"
  • "Can you see what the repayment is?"
Step 4 of 5

6 Steps of Closing

Move from recommendation to a booked outcome. Work through each step in order — don't skip to a commitment before the customer has picked a bank.

1 · Pick a bank

Ask which lender they prefer and get a tentative selection, even if they raise an objection first. Use AAPA if one comes up.

2 · Sell intellectually

Re-explain the savings (use the savings calculator if applicable). For purchases, show how extra repayments or an offset/redraw pay the loan off sooner.

3 · Sell emotionally

Link back to the why — build urgency and excitement.

BROKER"...the sooner we can get you pre-approved, the sooner you can make an offer, and we can get you out of renting and paying for someone else's mortgage."

4 · Gain a commitment

BROKER"So just want to confirm — you're happy with XYZ Bank which we have presented? If you change your mind we can always change to another lender."

5 · VVDR

VOI, Valuation, Documents, Referrals.

6 · Close with an agreed outcome

Ensure BBAs are booked within 48 hours.

Closing by intent level

Low intent
"I will tentatively select XYZ Bank for now and we can change to another lender if you change your mind by our next appointment. I'll still need you to upload your documents before our next appointment so I can review the servicing — so if you could please upload them by the morning of the appointment day, I will make sure I have all the final numbers ready for you."
High intent

Pitch Live Docs / same-day submission

"Here at Aussie we actually offer a same day submission (within 24 hours) — would you be able to quickly jump on a video call with me now and we can finalize the details and I can get the application underway now."
⚡Hitting an objection mid-close? Open the Objection handling panel — the closing objections live under their own tab there.
Step 5 of 5

Follow Up

Every follow-up call needs a purpose — re-sell and link back to why the customer reached out in the first place.

⏱BBA needs to be booked within 48 hours — the further out it's booked, the more customer intent drops off.

Call cadence

Minimum 3 attempts, 1–2 days apart
1

Attempt 1

Call at the time of the BBA. No show → leave a voice message.

2

Attempt 2

Call — if no answer, leave a voice message and send an email reminder.

3

Attempt 3

Call — if no answer, leave a voice message advising you'll close their file, and to reach back out by phone or email when ready.

Documents issue

Script
BROKER"I see that you haven't been able to provide the documents for me yet — I can see there's only a couple of things left. I can re-send you the link now, can you please upload while I am on the phone with you so we can get it out of the way." "Ideally we want to get your application to the bank ASAP to avoid the long queue and get you the approval ASAP, so you can start saving / purchase the property / get rid of those debts." "I don't want to waste your time with these follow up calls, and I know you don't want to waste my time either — so if you can't provide any documents to me by [X date], I will close off your file and you can reach back out to me when you have more time."

Intent issue — still thinking about it

Script
BROKER"As I have discussed there is definitely savings to be made — which part of the process, or which lender, is holding you back?"
